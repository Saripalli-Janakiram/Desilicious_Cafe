About Us

Welcome to Desilicious Cafe, your neighborhood haven for authentic, 100% vegetarian South Indian breakfast. Located on West Parmer Line, Austin Texas, we bring you the comfort and flavor of home-cooked classics like idly, vada, dosa, pongal, poori, and chapathi — made fresh every day with love and tradition. Whether you're craving a quick bite or a leisurely meal with family, we invite you to savor the warmth, spice, and soul of South Indian cuisine — right here in Austin.
